  • General questions

  • Where do I place an order?

    Zupply is Zalando's internal indirect procurement tool for all purchase orders. Due to our legal and contract terms you are committed to place each order via this tool.

    For a Zupply onboarding please contact inpro.marketing@zalando.de.

  • What's a service period?

    The service period is the time in which the ordered activity was performed.

    Example: Booking model for photo shoot
    - Modeling in photo shoot takes place in May
    - Invoice is issued in July.
    - Zalando settles invoice in August. May is the service period and month to report these costs.

  • My login for a CoCo tool is not working.

    Some CoCo tools might need a working VPN connection. If your VPN is working fine and you still experience issues connecting to one of our tools, please reach out to CoCo and we are happy to look into the error.

  • What happens if I am on holidays ?

    Please make sure to set up a deputy/substitute for Maxflow and Zupply. In case you will be OoO for the cost reporting please make sure to also inform CoCo and name a representative.

  • What are accruals?

    Accrual is an accounting method that records revenues and expenses when they are incurred, regardless of when cash is exchanged. At Zalando accruals have to be reported every month and are registered at the service period.

  • What are actuals?

    Actuals are the final costs that are booked by accounting when the invoice is received. (Clue: Things that have happened rather than things that are planned.

  • Do I have to report donations?

    Since donations are not processed via an invoice, please DO NOT report any donations to us, but remember donations are still part of your yearly marketing budget.

  • What are Commodity/product groups in Zupply ?

    Indirect procurement is distinguishing between different commodities and product groups to separate costs. Please make sure to always stay within the Marketing commodity and choose the touchpoint that is closest to your ordered service. Also make sure to pick the right accounting information before you submit your final order, these include the right cost type, segment and order ID (touchpoint).

  • What are intercompany costs and do I have to report them?

    Intercompany accounting involves recording financial transactions between different legal entities within the same parent company (parent: Zalando; subsidiary company: ZMS/Collabary/Z Studios). Because these entities are related, the transactions between them are not independent and companies cant include a profit or loss from these transactions on consolidated financial statements. Common scenarios include sales and purchases of services and goods between a parent company and its subsidiaries, fee sharing, cost allocations, royalties, and financing activities.

    Please DO NOT report these costs to us!

  • What are internal sales orders and do I have to report them?

    Internal Sales are a diferent payment method to pay up a partner. This can include a payment by a gift card/voucher or goodies such as clothing.

    Please DO NOT report these costs to us!

  • What are touchpoints ?

    With touchpoints we differentiate certain activities on our marketing cost types. These include TV, Radio, Influencer Marketing, PR, Events, Tooling Fees, and so on. Do not hesitate to have a look at our care package.

  • What are the different deadlines ?

    There are different topics with different deadlines.

    For the cost reporting process the deadline is the 1st business day of each month at 12PM (noon) to submit your costs.
    For the budget planning, review phases and accruals weeks CoCo will always share a detailed timeline and roadmap with you. This will happen several weeks in advance to guarantee you are always in the loop and aware of our deadlines.

  • WSB / PSP Generator

  • What is a PSP ?

    With the help of a PSP (Project Structured Plan) or WBS (Work Breakdown Structure) we can track and break down all costs on a campaign level.

  • How does the PSP/WBS generator work?

    To use the PSP generator you just have to select the different elements of the campaign: year, campaign, channel and sub- channel.
    Then click on "Generate PSP" in the tool. The associated PSP will be automatically displayed.
    You can then copy/paste the created element for further usage.

  • How can I create a new PSP/WBS?

    If you want to create a new PSP element you can just contact your point of contact in CoCo team. We'll let you know once this is created.

  • Budget Planning

  • What is Budget Planning ?

    Zalando has a yearly budget plan. The budget planning is a process in which we define long term goals and guidelines. There will be review rounds to refine this early forecast.
    The full budget cycle is monitored and managed by the Cost Controlling team. This means we will make sure that you are aware of deadlines and that you receive the right inputs at the right time.

  • GMC Forecasting

  • What is GMC forecasting ?

    The forecasting process is an ongoing procedure and completes the planning cycle of Zalando. At any time it shows the latest status of planned budgets.
    The information is used for regular reporting and steering meetings and enables you to manage resources in the most effective way.

  • What is the deadline to submit the forecast ?

    The forecasting process is an ongoing procedure and completes the planning cycle of Zalando. At any time it shows the latest status of planned budgets.
    Yet, every Thursday this forecast is shared with different stakeholders and therefore always have to reflect the most up-to-date picture.

  • Cost Reporting

  • Why do I have to report costs ?

    This is a process that gives an overview of what has been spent in the previous month, relevant for accounting and for monitoring expenses. It reports actual costs that happened the previous month or quarter. This is due the first bursiness day of every month.

  • What is the deadline for cost reporting?

    Whenever a month is over, the monthly cost reporting takes place. The first working day of the new month is the deadline for delivering the input. The data for the Fashion Store Marketing is collected per Touchpoint and country and serves as the baseline for the accrual building process.

  • Cost Overviews

  • What are Cost Overviews?

    The Cost Overview is your #1 solution in the SAP Analytics Cloud to check on your reported costs (= built accruals) and all actual costs (booked invoices).

  • What's happening during the Accruals Week?

    During the Accruals Week you will have to comment on all remaining accruals, that we have still kept aside for you. With the help of the SAC Cost Overviews and your own invoice tracking you can check an all invoices that have been already processed. In case an invoice is still missing, you would have to provide us with that information in form of a commentation, if we still need these accruals or can release them.

  • How do I check the status of an Invoice?

    Open your SAC Cost Overview and check the "Invoices" tab. You can use the filter option to either search for a specific partner, service period, touchpoint, etc. Once you found the invoice, you already know it has been booked to our internal SAP system. With the help of the document number you can then easily check on the payment status with the help of the Google Hangouts bot ChriZ. If you still can't find what you are looking for, please contact CoCo.
